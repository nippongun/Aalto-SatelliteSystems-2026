# Requirements Analysis

## Objectives

AtmoSat-1 is driven by four top-level objectives derived from the mission need (monitoring conflict-driven atmospheric chemistry change from a 3U CubeSat in SSO).

| ID | Objective |
|----|-----------|
| OBJ-01 | Monitor atmospheric ozone column and SWIR-band combustion anomalies over conflict and reference regions in Europe |
| OBJ-02 | Operate from a 3U CubeSat platform in Sun-Synchronous Orbit at 500 km altitude |
| OBJ-03 | Deliver calibrated science data products to users within 24 hours of acquisition |
| OBJ-04 | Comply with all legal, regulatory, and launch provider requirements for satellite operation |

## Requirements Hierarchy

The hierarchy flows from mission objectives down through mission requirements, observation requirements, and instrument requirements. Science objectives (OBJ-01) decompose into measurement performance requirements which further decompose into observation and instrument requirements. Platform, data delivery, and compliance objectives (OBJ-02–04) decompose directly into mission-level requirements with no observation sub-layer.

```
Objectives and Drivers
├── OBJ-01  Atmospheric monitoring
│   ├── REQ-01  Ozone detection (≥ 5 DU)
│   │   ├── OBS-01  UV channel spectral coverage (270–340 nm, ≤ 1.5 nm FWHM)
│   │   └── OBS-02  UV SNR ≥ 100 at 305 nm
│   ├── REQ-02  SWIR anomaly detection (≥ 5% relative radiance change) [secondary]
│   │   ├── OBS-03  SWIR anomaly channel (2.28–2.34 µm, ≤ 15 nm FWHM, radiometric repeatability)
│   │   └── OBS-04  SWIR SNR ≥ 200 at 2.31 µm [TBD: radiometric analysis at PDR]
│   ├── REQ-03  Swath ≥ 150 km
│   ├── REQ-04  Revisit ≤ 14 days at 50° N
│   ├── REQ-05  Geolocation ≤ 10 km (1σ)
│   └── REQ-06  Radiometric calibration ≤ 3% absolute
├── OBJ-02  3U CubeSat platform / SSO 500 km
│   ├── REQ-07  Mass ≤ 4 kg, envelope 10×10×30 cm
│   ├── REQ-08  Operational lifetime ≥ 90 days
│   ├── REQ-09  Payload power ≥ 3 W (sunlit)
│   ├── REQ-10  Nadir pointing ≤ 1° (3σ)
│   └── REQ-11  Orbital lifetime ≤ 25 years post-EOM
├── OBJ-03  Data delivery within 24 hours
│   ├── REQ-12  Downlink within 24 h of acquisition
│   ├── REQ-13  Downlink rate ≥ 1 Mbps at 750 km slant range [TBD: frequency band]
│   └── REQ-14  On-board storage ≥ 24 h science data
└── OBJ-04  Legal and regulatory compliance
    ├── REQ-15  Finnish Ministry of Economic Affairs authorisation
    ├── REQ-16  FICORA/ITU radio frequency licence
    ├── REQ-17  Launch provider vibration qualification test
    └── REQ-18  ESA debris mitigation (≤ 25 years post-EOM)
```
